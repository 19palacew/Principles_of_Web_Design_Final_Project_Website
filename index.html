Welcome to my Portfolio Website

 On this site I will be displaying my various projects and activities that I have worked on for both professional and personal purposes. The projects tab contains programming projects. Some projects were done out of a personal interest whereas others were done for school such as Trivia Maze.
 Games contains mostly games made for the University of Washington Game Development Club. We put on regular game jams for the members of our club and even do a collaboration with the other campus clubs over the summer for our Tri-Campus Game Jam.
 Visual Arts is some of the 3D design and render work that I do. I find that in working with games, it is important to get a good understanding of how the design process for creating game assets works. My visual arts section shows some of the render work I have done.
