Robo Rally

Robo Rally was a game built with the Unity Game Engine for the Winter 2022 Game Jam at UWT. The game was a third person 3D game where the player had to avoid enemies and reach the goal throughout a basic map. Aside from the engine being made by Unity, all of the other assets (including the music) were made by me or one of my teammates. I made all of the models in Blender and used Unity to bake the lighting. Most of the programming was also done by myself, I took care of the movement and enemy AI.
